Research Interests

Multimodal Machine Learning

I believe a necessary ability for machines to achieve human-level understanding about the world is to process various modalities simultaneously. Currently I am working on multimodal machine learning at MARS Lab advised by Prof. Hang Zhao, especially investigating novel application of thermal imaging.

Sports Analytics

Particularly, I am interested in machine learning methods for team sports analytics. The increasing amount of event and tracking data about team sports allows machine learning (especially deep learning) to be applied in this field. It is my belief that advanced machine learning methods will help improve team sports tactics in a way hard for human to discover. Currently I am working on this area in Prof. Shaoliang Zhang’s group.
